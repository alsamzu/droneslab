AD1 | TRAINING UAS

WORKING DRONE

Not your average drone. Designed for commercial and industrial sectors, the AD1 is a multi-purpose aerial platform, packed with customizable features. Its distinct low-profile, foldable compact and lightweight design, compliments a variety of applications.

STANDARD FEATURES
  • Portable and compact airframe
  • Payload 4.8 lbs (2.18 kg)
  • Failsafe, return to home functions
  • Collapsible folding arms
  • Ground Control Station
  • Autonomous Flight Control

AD2 | INDUSTRIAL UAS

INDUSTRIAL GRADE DRONE

Enterprise platform for longer flight times. Designed for heavy lifting. Payloads can be configured for mapping, surveying, creative or inspection work, specific to a drone’s mission goals.

AD2 PERFORMANCE SPECIFICATIONS

Size: 38.6 in (980 mm) diagonal Propellers:18 in. carbon fiber hollow core Flight Time:30-45 minutes Flight Controller:Pixhawk (recommended), A2, or user choice Batteries: 12,000 mAh – 17,000 total mAh, 6s LiPo Modes: Autonomous GPS, Manual, Attitude Telemetry Range:1 mi (1.6 km) Wind Tolerance:25 mph (40.2 km/h)

STRUCTURE INSPECTIONS

Everyday, technician inspectors can be injured on the job. Benefitting industries (but not limited to), are power, electric, wind turbine farms, dams, bridge maintenance and repair, construction developers, as well as rooftop and solar panel companies. Utilizing our drone systems and programs, inspectors and surveyors can navigate and inspect risky locations in real time.

EDUCATION

The Arts have made it’s way into STEAM afterschool programs. It has become a fulfilling passion for AD to encourage students about drones while investing back into youth community projects and school programs.

PRECISION AGRICULTURE

Drone aerial survey missions mapping and technology can efficiently help farmers save time and energy, producing higher crop yield, quality and cost savings. SLANTRANGE® sensors and analytics provide entirely new metrics on crop status, health trajectory, or projected performance.

PUBLIC SAFETY & SECURITY

Already, there have been an increasing interest for the use of drones in the law enforcement agencies, border patrol, fire departments, search and rescue teams. Mobile drones can instantly provide close range live video feed, efficiently minimizing harmful risks and saving lives.

MAPPING & SURVEYING

To minimize “boots on the ground,” our integrated V-MAP® drone systems systematically map with 2 cm accuracy. Generate highly accurate, high resolution orthomaps and 3D volumetric models without having to capture ground control points. Surveyors can benefit for construction sites, as well as geologists and natural disaster damage surveillance.

CREATIVE

Aerial video and photography allow for unique shots and perspectives. Among the needed industries, real estate photographers, agents, property managers, developers and inspectors, benefit from aerial imagery useful for buyers, sellers, and investors.
